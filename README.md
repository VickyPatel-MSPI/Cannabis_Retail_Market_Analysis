Since cannabis legalization in Canada, retail store distribution has expanded unevenly across provinces and cities.

This project aims to answer the following business questions:

**ğŸ” Key Insights**

- Ontario leads with the highest number of cannabis retail stores

---

**ğŸ¯ Conclusion**

The analysis reveals that cannabis retail presence in Canada is concentrated in a limited number of leading provinces, with significant clustering in major urban centers.

High-ranking provinces exhibit signs of competitive saturation, while lower-ranked regions demonstrate comparatively lower retail penetration and potential growth opportunities.

Overall, the data indicates a mature and operational cannabis retail environment with uneven geographic distribution across the country.
